About Me

Nishita Gohel is a junior at Michigan State University studying Media and Information. Her goals after she graduates is to get into a Dental School to become an Orthodontist. During her free time, Nishi loves to travel, listen to music, be outdoors, dance, and hang out with friends.

One of Nishi's favorite hobbies is to dance. She is on an Indian dance team at MSU (MSU Raas Team), and has gotten first place at competitions in An Arbor and in Maryland in the past two weeks. Her dance team has also placed at competitions in Indiana and Pennsylvania.

Nishi also loves to spend time with her family. Every summer, she goes on a hiking trip with all her aunts, uncles, and cousins for a week. Spending time with her family is one of her favorite ways to escape her everyday life. While on these trips, she loves to go kayaking, rafting, biking, playing sports with her neices and nephews, and going swimming.
